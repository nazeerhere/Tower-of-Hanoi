Learn to Play:

Click on the red disk and move it to any of the other two towers to start the game. The disk are arranged in rainbow order- ROYGBIV, with white and black being the last two colors, if 8 disk are selected. The goal of the puzzle is to move all the disks from the leftmost peg to the rightmost peg, adhering to the following rules:

  1. Move only one disk at a time.
  2. The disk may not be placed out of order (e.g., since red will always be the color on top it can be placed on any other disk, but none of the other disk can be placed on top of red.)
  3. All disks, except the one being moved, must be on a peg.

Side note: If you're having trouble finding where to click, toggle the little brown "i" button under the towers. A back screen will show where you can click to add the disk.
